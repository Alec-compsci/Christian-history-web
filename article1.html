What is the Gospel (Good News)

Incase you're curious, an essential aspect of Christianity is the Gospel, or the Good News. Which is although we all deserve eternal hell, by God's love and grace he came in the flesh as the Messiah Jesus Chrst and died on the cross to take on the punishment of our sins. Then he was ressurected three days later, then later ascended back into heaven. This means only through faith in him can we be saved and made righteous.
